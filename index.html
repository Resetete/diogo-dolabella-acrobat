About me

Diogo Dolabella is originally from Brazil and has completed his art education at circus schools in Brazil (SPASSO - Belo Horizonte) and Belgium (ESAC - Brussels). Diogo has extensively performed with some well-known circus artists across Europe, North and South America and Asia. Among others, He has worked with directors and companies such as Franco Dragone, Chamaleon Theater, Palazzo, GOP and many others. His incredible performances have been seen in many Festivals around the world. He also works as a teacher at Die Etage and Cabuwazi in Berlin.

He extensively performed with circus well-known artists worldwide especially across Europe, North and South America. He performed with Chamaleon Theater, Palazzo and GOP.
